Perfect Lasagna

Nothing beats classic lasagna. And this is the easiest recipe you will ever make. It can be made ahead and it’s freezer-friendly too!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 on onion diced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 (15-ounce) package whole milk ricotta
  • 3 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella, divided
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup grated paresan
  • 1/4 cup parsley

Directions

  1. Heat oven 350F Oil baking dish w nonstick spray
  2. Large pot boil saslted water, cook noodles to package
  3. Heat olive oil in large skillet, add ground beef until brown (3-5 mins), salt pepper, drain fat, stir in tomatoes, and seasoning
  4. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella and egg; set aside. Spread 1 cup tomato mixture onto the bottom of a 9×13 baking dish; top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Repeat with a second layer. Top with remaining noodles, tomato mixture, 1 cup mozzarella cheese and Parmesan.*
  5. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired.
